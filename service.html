Frequently asked questions

Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) you can include on an interior design service page:

3D design is the creation of realistic, three-dimensional models using specialized software to represent objects or spaces. It helps visualize ideas with depth, texture, and lighting before physical implementation, ensuring a more accurate and detailed outcome.

The cost of interior design can vary greatly depending on factors such as the size of the space, complexity of the project, location, and the designer's experience. Typically, interior design services can be charged in several ways, including hourly rates, flat fees, or a percentage of the overall project cost, with prices ranging from a few thousand to tens of thousands of dollars.

The time spent on planning an interior design project can vary depending on the complexity and scope of the project. Generally, for a residential design, you may spend 2 to 4 weeks on planning, including consultations, brainstorming, and finalizing the design concept. For larger or more detailed projects, such as commercial spaces, planning could take several weeks to a few months. The planning phase involves research, layout design, sourcing materials, and finalizing the overall vision.

Yes, you can create a custom design! Custom interior design allows you to incorporate your personal style, preferences, and specific needs into the space. Whether it's for a residential or commercial project, custom designs are tailored to your unique vision, from layout and furniture to colors, materials, and lighting. A professional designer can work closely with you to bring your ideas to life and ensure the final result reflects your personality and functionality requirements.
